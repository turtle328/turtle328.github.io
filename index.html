Game of Life | March 2020

Game of Life is a universe where tiny things can take over the world. The starting pattern will always result in the same behavior across the generations. However the slightest changes in the starting pattern can determine whether the pattern stabalizes after 5 generatons or 5000 generations. This app has been preloaded with plenty of patterns for you to try out! The canvas can be interacted with any time to create lifes with mouse click/drag, holding shift erases the lifes instead. One of my favorite features of the app is the Wrap toggle, which allows the lifes to wrap across the screen giving wildly different results from patterns, be sure to try it out! Developed in HTML/CSS/JavaScript.

Snowglobe Dream | December 2019

Worked with 4 other partners: Brad Hanel, Eddie Brazier, Jack Watson, Nick Schnering. The five of us were tasked with making a scene in Unity with a unique atmosphere and at least 15 handcrafted models made in Maya. We collectively decided on a christmasy theme where you are trapped inside a snowglobe. We each did about four models. The four I were responsible for was the chair, table, bed, and one of the three snowman models. Creating each model is a process: modeling, UV unwrapping, and texturing. We worked excellently as a team and I am really proud of how this project turned out! It also gave me experience working with Git and how to deal with forking and merges, and how to deal with the merge conflicts when they arise.
